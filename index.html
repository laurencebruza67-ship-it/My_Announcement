PUBLIC ADVISORY

River Flood Warning in force for Gulayan areas

Thursday 02 July 2026

RIVER FLOOD WARNING


All residents in low-lying and flood-prone areas near the Gulayan River are strongly urged to evacuate immediately to higher ground or the nearest evacuation centre. River levels continue to rise and are now above warning levels, posing a serious risk to communities along these rivers and downstream floodplain areas.

A Flood Alert is also in force for Gulayan, with the situation expected to worsen as we approach high tide around 10:12pm tonight, combined with the delayed impacts of heavy rainfall over the past 24 hours. The National Disaster Risk Management Office (NDRMO) advises communities in these areas to remain on high alert and be prepared to move without delay if conditions deteriorate.

For assistance or evacuation information, contact your nearest Divisional Emergency Operations Centre.
